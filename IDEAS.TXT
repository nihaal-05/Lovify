1. THE ENTRY & INVITE RITUAL (THE SOUL-LINK)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
- SOUL-PROFILER: AI quiz to build the partner’s "Soul Profile."
- THE GOLDEN TICKET: Generate a 3D Golden Envelope to invite the partner.
- PAIRING ANIMATION: When they join, hearts fuse in a Kintsugi flash.
- LOGIN: 3D Heart splits like curtains to reveal the Dashboard.

2. THE "LOVE CINEMA" (WEEKLY MOVIE RECAP)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
- FEATURE: Every Sunday (or on-demand), the app generates a high-quality 
  mini-movie of the week.
- CONTENT: Clips from video calls, photos from the Memory Vault, 
  "Top Flirty Lines," and total "Time Spent" milestones.
- ANIMATION: A cinematic slideshow with "Parallax" effects on photos, 
  synced to "Our Song" (Audio-Haptic Sync).

3. VISIBILITY: PRIVATE VS. PUBLIC MODES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
- PRIVATE MODE (The Sanctuary): 
  - Visual: Midnight Indigo theme with frosted glass. 
  - Privacy: Data stays strictly between the two users. No global ranking.
- PUBLIC MODE (The Hall of Flame): 
  - Feature: A global leaderboard where couples show off their 
    "Bond Score" and "Adventure Path" progress.
  - Social: Shared "Milestone Cards" can be exported to other platforms.

4. DAILY RITUALS & COMMUNICATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
- NIGHT-TIME GRATITUDE: App locks until a "Thank You" note is swapped.
- ROMANTIC CHAT: Jelly-physics bubbles, Secret Notes, and "The Dissolve."
- SECRET LANGUAGE: Live "Golden Ink" symbols drawn on-screen.
- IMMERSIVE VIDEO: Golden Hour filters and real-time Pulse Sync.

5. CONFLICT & SAFETY MODULES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
- COOL-DOWN FREEZE: AI "Ices" the chat if tension is detected.
- APOLOGY BRIDGE: Guided prompts to reconnect broken 3D paths.
- THE ORACLE: AI bot for difficult relationship/life decisions.

6. SENSORY & PRESENCE FEATURES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
- LONELY MODE: Soft Fog + Candlelight icon on the partner's screen.
- SOFT-PING: Home-screen widget for non-verbal "thinking of you" taps.
- THE STAR-MAP: 3D Constellation of GPS-tagged moments.
- ADVENTURE PATH: Fog-of-war city map that clears as you explore.

7. THE RESOLUTION & REDEMPTION (BREAK-UP)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
- THE MUTUAL SHATTER: Heart turns to Stone and SHATTERS into particles.
- THE MEMORY MIRROR: A final sepia scroll of memories to prompt reflection.
- THE KINTSUGI REBUILD: If both agree, the heart fuses back with 
  Glowing Gold Seams, representing a stronger, repaired bond.

8. TECHNICAL ARCHITECTURE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Framework: Next.js 15 | Three.js | GSAP | Agora SDK.
- AI Logic: Gemini API for Sentiment, Oracle, and Weekly Movie Scripting.
- Haptics: WebVibration API for Pulse Sync and Movie Audio-Haptics.
