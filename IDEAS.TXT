1. THE ENTRY: AUTHENTICATION & ONBOARDING
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
A. THE HEART-BREAK LOGIN
   - Visual: A high-fidelity, glowing 3D Heart (Three.js) in center screen.
   - Interaction: User enters credentials.
   - Success Animation: The heart develops a vertical fracture. Using 
     GSAP, the two halves rotate outward and slide to the screen edges, 
     acting as curtains that reveal the main Dashboard.
   - Fail Animation: Heart shakes (haptic feedback) and dims to grey.

B. THE SOUL-PROFILER (ONBOARDING)
   - Steps: Select "He/She/They" -> Enter Partner Birthday.
   - Interaction: An AI-driven chat quiz profiling the partner's soul.
   - Animation: A "Progress Vine" grows and blooms flowers across the 
     bottom of the screen as questions are completed.

2. THE CENTRAL HUB (DASHBOARD)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
A. THE DUAL-BOND CALCULATOR
   - Engine 1 (Activity): Days + Chats + Calls + GPS Check-ins.
   - Engine 2 (Growth): Gratitude Streaks + Values Aligned + Resolution.
   - Main Visual: A 3D Liquid-filled Heart. Activity makes the liquid 
     bubble; Growth makes a "Life-Vine" wrap around the exterior.

B. LONELY MODE (THE PRESENCE ALERT)
   - Action: User taps "I feel lonely."
   - Partner Animation: Their screen develops a soft "Warm Fog" and a 
     single flickering candle icon appears. The "Meetup Map" highlights 
     cozy date spots nearby.

4. COMMUNICATION & PRIVACY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
- LOVIFY CHAT: "Jelly Physics" bubbles + "Secret Notes" (Tap to unblur).
- PEACE MODE (Veil): A misty overlay covers UI; stops all notifications.
- INCOGNITO WIPE: Messages turn to dust and blow away (The Dissolve).
- BIRTHDAY ENGINE: 1-week countdown begins; background rains confetti.

5. THE RESOLUTION MODULE (STRIFE)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
A. THE APOLOGY BRIDGE
   - Action: Triggered during conflict.
   - Interaction: Both users must complete "I feel..." prompts.
   - Animation: Two broken paths slowly move together to form a bridge. 
     Once they touch, the chat unlocks.

6. THE FINALITY: MUTUAL DISSOLUTION (BREAK-UP)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
Lovify treats the end with respect. A breakup cannot be impulsive.

A. THE INITIATION
   - Action: One user requests a "Reset."
   - Visual: The main 3D Heart begins to flicker and lose its color.

B. THE TWO-KEY RULE
   - Action: Both users must confirm the decision within a 24-hour window.

C. THE SAD ANIMATION (THE END)
   - Effect: Once both confirm, the heart turns to Cold Stone.
   - Final Action: The stone heart develops deep cracks and SHATTERS.
   - Animation: Using a physics-based particle system, the fragments 
     fall to the bottom of the screen and dissolve into grey smoke.
   - Result: All shared data is wiped or archived; Bond Score resets.

7. TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Framework: Next.js 15 (React)
- 3D/Animation: Three.js (R3F), GSAP, Framer Motion
- Backend/Real-time: Supabase (Realtime Channels)
- AI: Gemini API (Oracle/Sentiment Analysis)
- Maps: Mapbox API (Memory Vault/Adventure Path)
- Haptics: WebVibration API (Pulse Sync)
