1. THE ENTRY: AUTHENTICATION & ONBOARDING
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
A. THE HEART-BREAK LOGIN
   - Visual: A high-fidelity, glowing 3D Heart in center screen.
   - Success Animation: The heart vertical fractures and slides apart
     like curtains to reveal the Dashboard.

B. THE SOUL-PROFILER (ONBOARDING)
   - Action: AI-driven quiz profiling the partner's soul and birthday.
   - Animation: A "Progress Vine" blooms across the bottom.

2. CONFLICT & SAFETY MODULES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
A. THE "COOL-DOWN" FREEZE (AI DETECTION)
   - Feature: AI monitors chat for high-tension sentiment or aggression.
   - Action: Chat "Freezes" with a blue ice effect. Both users must 
     complete a "Sync-Breathing" exercise to unlock.

B. THE APOLOGY BRIDGE
   - Action: Guided "I feel..." prompts to resolve lingering tension.

3. THE DISSOLUTION PROTOCOL (THE BREAK-UP)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
A. THE FINALITY SHATTER
   - Initiation: One user requests a Reset; Heart begins to flicker.
   - The Two-Key Rule: Both must confirm within 24 hours.
   - Animation: The heart turns to Cold Stone, cracks, and SHATTERS into 
     grey particles that fall and dissolve into smoke.

B. THE MEMORY MIRROR (THE LAST GLANCE)
   - Interaction: As the heart shatters, the background reveals a 
     faded, sepia-toned version of the "Memory Vault."
   - Action: A final auto-generated message appears: "Before you go, 
     remember the 452 moments you built together. Is this truly the end?"
   - Visual: Highlights of their highest "Bond Score" moments and most 
     visited "Meetup Places" scroll slowly in the background.

4. THE RECONCILIATION & REBUILDING
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
A. THE REBUILD REQUEST
   - Feature: If both users hesitate or log back in within a "Grace 
     Period," they can trigger a "Rebuild."
   - Action: An auto-generated "Rebuild Message" is sent, showcasing a 
     randomized "Perfect Memory" from their Vault to spark nostalgia.

B. THE REBUILDING ANIMATION (THE HEALING)
   - Trigger: Both users press "Rebuild" simultaneously.
   - Animation: The grey stone fragments on the floor begin to glow 
     with gold light (Kintsugi style). They fly back together, 
     fusing the heart with glowing gold seams.
   - Result: The Bond Score starts at a "Grace Level," and a new 
     "Growth Vine" begins to wrap around the repaired heart.

5. THE IMMERSIVE MEMORY VAULT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
- THE STAR-MAP: Every memory (Name + Location + Date) is a star.
- THE JOURNEY: Tapping a star triggers a "Warp Speed" transition to 
  the 3D map location where that moment happened.
