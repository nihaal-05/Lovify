                PROJECT LOVIFY: THE STRATEGIC BLUEPRINT
             "A Digital Revolution for Modern Relationships"

1. CORE CONCEPT & IDENTITY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
Name: Lovify
Mission: To provide a high-end, animated, and private ecosystem for 
         couples to grow, communicate, and gamify their bond.
Visual Aesthetic: Glassmorphism, soft pastels (Rose, Cream, Deep Crimson), 
                  and high-fidelity 3D physics-based animations.

2. THE ENTRY EXPERIENCE (USER JOURNEY)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
A. THE HEART-BREAK LOGIN
   - Visual: A large, glowing 3D heart in the center of the screen.
   - Action: Upon successful login, the heart physically splits into two.
   - Animation: Using GSAP/Framer Motion, the halves rotate and slide
     to the edges like curtains, revealing the inner dashboard.
   - Fail-Safe: If login fails, the heart shakes ("No") and dims to grey.

B. THE SOUL-PROFILER (ONBOARDING)
   - Step 1: Selection of identity (He/She/They) with animated avatars.
   - Step 2: Interactive AI Quiz. User answers questions about their 
     partner (Love Language, favorite aesthetic, hobbies, etc.).
   - Visual: A "Progress Vine" grows and blooms at the bottom of the 
     screen as the profile is completed.

3. DUAL-MODE PRIVACY ARCHITECTURE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
A. PUBLIC MODE (COMPETITION)
   - Feature: Hall of Flame (Leaderboard).
   - Logic: Couples compete globally based on their "Bond Score."
   - Visuals: Vibrant gold and glowing UI accents.

B. PRIVATE MODE (GHOST MODE)
   - Feature: Complete Cloaking. 
   - Logic: All data is encrypted; couple is removed from leaderboards.
   - Visuals: UI morphs into "Midnight Indigo" with soft silver accents.

4. THE "BOND SCORE" ALGORITHM
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
The Bond Score is a real-time gamified representation of the relationship.
Formula: Score = (Days * 10) + (ChatCount * 2) + (CallMins * 5) + (CheckIns * 50)

Visual Representation: 
- A central "Heart Container" on the dash with a Liquid Fill effect.
- The "Love Liquid" sloshes and glows based on daily activity levels.

6. THE COMMUNICATION SUITE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
A. LOVIFY CHAT
   - Physics: "Jelly" text bubbles that bounce when appearing.
   - Special: "Secret Notes" that stay blurred until tapped by the partner.

B. ROMANTIC VIDEO CALLS
   - Filters: Automatic "Golden Hour" or "Soft Candlelight" GLSL shaders.
   - Sync: Shared "Watch Together" mode for the Memory Vault.

7. TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS (RECOMMENDED STACK)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Frontend: Next.js 15 (React)
- Animation: GSAP + Framer Motion + Three.js (React Three Fiber)
- Styling: Tailwind CSS
- Real-Time: Supabase (PostgreSQL + Realtime Channels)
- Communication: Agora SDK (Voice/Video)
- Haptics: WebVibration API

8. THE REVOLUTIONARY "LEGACY MODE"
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
After a specific milestone (e.g., 5 years), the app compiles all logged 
data, memories, and score peaks into a cinematic "Relationship Movie," 
creating a digital heirloom that lasts forever.
